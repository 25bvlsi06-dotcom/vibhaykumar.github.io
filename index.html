Who I Am

I'm Vibhay Kumar, an Electronics Engineering student from Lucknow with a deep passion for semiconductor design and integrated circuit architecture. I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in VLSI Design & Technology from the Centre for Advanced Studies under AKTU.

My journey began with a strong foundation in Mathematics, Physics, and Logical Reasoning. I believe in disciplined self-learning and strategic preparation — qualities that helped me qualify the IMU CET with a national rank of #3395.

I'm also a published researcher — my paper was accepted at ICISSE 2026, the 1st International Conference on Innovations in Smart and Sustainable Engineering. I run a YouTube channel VLSI with Nano Node dedicated to VLSI education.

I'm driven by a vision to contribute to the global semiconductor industry — designing circuits that power the future.
